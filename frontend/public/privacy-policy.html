Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Task Delegation Tracker, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (stored encrypted)
  • Company information: Company/organisation name
  • Task data: Task descriptions, due dates, assigned persons, completion status, and scores that you enter
  • Contact information: WhatsApp phone numbers of stakeholders you add (used only to send task reminders)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the task delegation and tracking service
  • To send daily WhatsApp reminders about pending tasks (only to numbers you configure)
  • To process subscription payments via Razorpay
  • To show you dashboard statistics and performance reports

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Neon.tech PostgreSQL databases hosted in the cloud. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Razorpay: For processing subscription payments
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API): To send task reminder messages
  • Neon.tech: Database hosting provider
  • Render.com: Application hosting provider

5. WhatsApp Reminders

Phone numbers you add are used exclusively to send pending task reminders via WhatsApp Business API. We do not use these numbers for any marketing or other purposes.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

8. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your task data at any time (CSV export available)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email.
